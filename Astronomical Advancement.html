India makes an Astronomical Advancement

Indian astronomers discover 8 rare stars hotter than the Sun



Astronomers from the Pune-based National Centre for Radio Astrophysics (NCRA) said they've recently discovered eight radio stars that are hotter than the Sun. These stars belonging to a rare class called MRP, have been found using Giant Metrewave Radio Telescope (GMRT), NCRA said. They have unusually strong magnetic fields and emit bright radio pulses like a lighthouse, it added.
